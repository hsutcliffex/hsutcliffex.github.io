Computing contributions in society

- Computers have had a lot of important contributions within society.
- However, arguably the most important contribution computers have had is towards more social aspects.
- During the unexpected pandemic witnessed in 2020, society was only able to continue to function due to the advance in our technology.
- Families who were unable to see each other relied solely on such technology to continue communication,
- Not to mention, the education system was upheld by computer functioning to continue to be able to deliver knowledge to bright young minds.
- Though computers have had several important functions, one cannot deny the importance in such an aspect of society.
